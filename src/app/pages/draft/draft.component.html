Should you draft a Quarterback?

There are many situations in which is becomes clear that a change needs to be made at the quarterback position. Maybe the incumbent is underperforming, there is an exciting prospect entering the draft, the current Qb contract is expiring, etc. However, many times it is not clear, and teams hold on to their current QB's for far too long, and they miss out on prospects, or their team gets too expensive (the sport has a salary cap). This algorithm will not tell you if your decision is correct, it will tell you what other teams in a similar situation have done.
